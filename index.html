Smart Glasses Concept

Assistive technology prototype for the visually impaired, utilizing ATtiny85 and Ultrasonic Sensing.

1. Interactive 3D Prototype

Drag to rotate • Scroll to zoom

Form Factor: Sports Glasses. HC-SR04 positioned at the front bridge, with ATtiny85, Buzzer, and Battery routed inside the right and left temples.

Hardware & Pinout

Specifications

  • Microcontroller: ATtiny85 (DIP-8)
  • Sensor: HC-SR04 Ultrasonic
  • Output: Active Piezo Buzzer
  • Power: 5V (USB/Battery Pack)

Pinout Mapping

  • Pin 8 : VCC (Battery + & Sensor VCC)
  • Pin 4 : GND (Battery -, Sensor & Buzzer GND)
  • PB1 : Buzzer (+) OUTPUT
  • PB4 : Echo Sensor INPUT
  • PB3 : Trig Sensor OUTPUT
